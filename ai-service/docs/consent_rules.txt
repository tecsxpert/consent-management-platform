Consent Management Rules

Users must provide explicit consent before personal data is collected or processed. Consent requests must clearly explain what data is being collected and how it will be used.

Users should be able to withdraw consent at any time through accessible account settings or support channels. Withdrawal of consent must stop future data processing activities unless legally required otherwise.

Sensitive information such as financial data, biometric data, health records, and government identification numbers requires higher protection standards and additional user confirmation.

Third-party data sharing must be disclosed clearly in consent agreements. Users should be informed about external partners receiving their data and the purpose of such sharing.

Consent records must be securely stored for audit and compliance purposes. The platform should maintain timestamps and logs for all consent actions performed by users.

The platform should regularly review and update consent policies to remain compliant with privacy regulations and industry standards.
